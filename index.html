A Little About Something I Love: Baseball

The Following is a List of 5 Reason I Love Baseball (With Explanations):
  1. It is just classic!
    • There's nothing quite like the old-timey organ sounds
    • The pin-striped uniforms take the crowd back to simpler times
    • The atmosphere at the ballpark feels vintage and nostalgic
  2. The food is so good! It may be simple but there really is no beating it.
    • Hot Dogs
    • Popcorn
    • Pretzels
    • Sunflower Seeds
    • Endless other classic options!
  3. Once you understand the strategy involved, it is easily one of the most complex and interesting sports to watch!
  4. It can take you all over! Seriously, all over. I have seen my favorite team, the San Francisco Giants, in the following locations:
    • San Francisco
    • Atlanta
    • Miami
    • Pheonix
    • And counting!
  5. The overall experience is unbeatable
    • The sound of the crack of a bat
    • The smell of popcorn and freshly cut grass
    • The thrill of a homerun
